# Prototype 2016 Forecast Figure Captions

These figures are legacy/debug support only transport benchmark support visuals built from the stored benchmark rasters, with case-local drifter-centered geographic context, a small locator inset, exact stored raster cells, exact footprint outlines, and a provenance source-point star when that asset is available.

Guardrails:

- PyGNOME is comparator-only, not truth
- prototype_2016 p50/p90 are exact valid-time member-occupancy footprints, not pooled-particle-density thresholds
- transport benchmark only
- not final Chapter 3 evidence
